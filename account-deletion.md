# Delete Your Account

If you would like to delete your Sharky account and all associated data, you can do so in one of the following ways:

## Option 1 — In-app

1. Open Sharky
2. Tap **Account** in the bottom navigation
3. Scroll to the bottom and tap **Delete account**
4. Confirm the deletion

Your account and all associated data (saved beaches, preferences) will be permanently deleted immediately.

## Option 2 — Email request

Send a deletion request to **hello@sharkyapp.com** from the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.
