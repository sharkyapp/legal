# Sharky — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 20 March 2026**

**Version: 1.0**

---

## 1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Sebastiaan Korsten ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use the Sharky application and related services (the "Service").

We handle personal information in accordance with the *Privacy Act 1988* (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

---

## 2. What We Collect and Why

We collect only what is necessary to operate the Service.

### 2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

- **Email address** — to authenticate you and send sign-in links.
- **Name and profile picture** (Google sign-in only) — provided by Google when you choose to sign in with your Google account.

### 2.2 User Preferences

When you are signed in, we store:

- **Saved beaches** — the list of beaches you have added to your favourites.
- **Display preference** — your chosen light or dark theme.

These are saved to your account so your preferences carry across devices.

### 2.3 Location Data

If you grant location permission, your device's coordinates are used to sort and display nearby beaches. **Location data is processed on your device only and is never sent to or stored on our servers.**

You can revoke location permission at any time in your device settings. This will disable the nearby beaches feature but will not affect your access to the rest of the Service.

### 2.4 What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

- precise location history or movement data;
- payment or financial information;
- usage analytics or behavioural tracking data; or
- any information from users who do not create an account (the Service is usable without signing in).

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

- authenticate you and maintain your account;
- store and sync your saved beaches and display preferences across devices; and
- respond to support or feedback requests you send us.

We do not use your information for advertising, marketing, or profiling.

---

## 4. Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We use the following third-party service provider to operate the Service:

- **Supabase** (Supabase Inc.) — cloud database and authentication infrastructure. Your account information and preferences are stored on Supabase's servers. Supabase is bound by its own privacy and security commitments; see [supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy).

When you sign in with Google, Google processes your authentication in accordance with [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). We receive only the email address, name, and profile picture that Google provides.

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or a valid legal process.

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

Your account data and preferences are stored on servers located in Australia or the United States, operated by Supabase. We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access controls.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your account information and preferences for as long as your account exists. If you wish to have your data deleted, contact us at hello@sharkyapp.com.au and we will delete your account and associated data.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to:

- **access** the personal information we hold about you;
- **correct** information that is inaccurate or out of date; and
- **request deletion** of your personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@sharkyapp.com.au.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. We will notify registered users of material changes via email or in-app notice where practicable.
